About Us

We want to find coffee lovers worldwide

Our Story

Colombia is full of hard-working people, whom every day aim to be better to have a better future, and some of them have found a great, unique, and amazing way to do it through working with coffee.

What a fantastic product to work with, which offers these people new opportunities, new beginnings, and ways to be recognized worldwide because of the remarkable and particular product.

We are a brand of coffee growers, businessmen, artists, and people who have big hearts and are passionate about what we do.

Colombia is full of people who believe we can achieve our goals thanks to the people surrounding us. That is why we want to support all the Colombian talent that is making a difference in other countries.

Our Vision

The first thing we want to transmit with our brand is High-quality Colombian Coffee with good social and environmental responsibility.

Attributes: Quality, Prestige, Ecological, Colombian, Social, Semi-artisanal, Semi-industrial.

Benefits: Gratitude, Status, Health, Energy, Wellness, Science, and Art.

Products: High-quality coffee. A selected product with high traceability.

Identity inspired by the social transformation of the city of Medellín. One of the three most dangerous cities in the world, and now, one of the most innovative cities. Resignify the word Escobar. Create a new referent in the minds. Support entities with good labor practices and support regenerative initiatives.
